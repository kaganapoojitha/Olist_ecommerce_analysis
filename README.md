## 🛒 Olist E-Commerce Sales Analysis | SQL

### Tools: PostgreSQL, PowerBI

### Business Problem
Analyze 100K+ orders to identify revenue trends,
top categories, and delivery performance.

### Key Findings
- Total revenue: $16M across 2016-2018
- Revenue grew 22% from 2017 to 2018
- Health & Beauty is #1 revenue category
- São Paulo accounts for 42% of all orders
- Credit card dominates at 78% of payments
- 97% order delivery success rate
- Peak sales in November 2017 (Black Friday)
- Avg delivery time: 12.6 days

### Recommendations
1. Focus marketing on SP, RJ, MG — top 3 states
2. Stock up Health & Beauty before November
3. Offer credit card EMI options to boost AOV
4. Reduce delivery time below 10 days to improve satisfaction
